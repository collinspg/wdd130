Services & Portfolio

SERVICES: At Kolie's Integrated Services, we offer a comprehensive range of electrical services to meet the needs of our valued clients. Our team of skilled electricians is experienced in handling various projects, from residential to commercial and industrial. Some of our key services include:

1. ELECTRICAL INSTALLATION: We specialize in professional electrical installations for new construction projects, renovations, and upgrades. From wiring and lighting to power distribution systems, we ensure safe and efficient electrical installations tailored to your requirements.

2. ELECTRICAL REPAIRS: Our expert technicians are proficient in diagnosing and resolving electrical issues promptly. Whether you're experiencing faulty wiring, power outages, or malfunctioning fixtures, we provide reliable electrical repair services to restore the functionality of your electrical systems.

3. ELECTRICAL MAINTENACE: Regular maintenance is crucial for the longevity and optimal performance of your electrical systems. Our team offers comprehensive electrical maintenance services, including inspections, testing, and preventive measures, to keep your systems safe, efficient, and compliant with regulations.

Faqs

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS.

Q: Are you licensed and insured? A: Yes, we are fully licensed and insured. Our team of electricians is trained and certified to handle a wide range of electrical projects safely and effectively.

Q: Do you offer emergency electrical services? A: Absolutely. We understand that electrical emergencies can occur at any time. We provide 24/7 emergency electrical services to address urgent issues promptly. Please call our emergency hotline for immediate assistance.

Q: What areas do you serve? A: We proudly serve Lagos Nigeria, and the surrounding regions. Please contact us to confirm if we provide services in your specific location.

Q: Can you handle both residential and commercial electrical projects? A: Yes, we have extensive experience in both residential and commercial electrical projects. Our team is equipped to handle a diverse range of electrical services for homes, businesses, and industrial facilities.

Q: What payment options do you accept? A: We accept various payment options, including cash, check, and major credit cards. We will provide detailed payment information and options when discussing your project or service request.
