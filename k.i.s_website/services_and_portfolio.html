SERVICES

At Kolie's Integrated Services, we offer a comprehensive range of electrical services to meet the needs of our valued clients. Our team of skilled electricians is experienced in handling various projects, from residential to commercial and industrial. Some of our key services include:

1. ELECTRICAL INSTALLATION: We specialize in professional electrical installations for new construction projects, renovations, and upgrades. From wiring and lighting to power distribution systems, we ensure safe and efficient electrical installations tailored to your requirements.

2. ELECTRICAL REPAIRS: Our expert technicians are proficient in diagnosing and resolving electrical issues promptly. Whether you're experiencing faulty wiring, power outages, or malfunctioning fixtures, we provide reliable electrical repair services to restore the functionality of your electrical systems.

3. ELECTRICAL MAINTENACE: Regular maintenance is crucial for the longevity and optimal performance of your electrical systems. Our team offers comprehensive electrical maintenance services, including inspections, testing, and preventive measures, to keep your systems safe, efficient, and compliant with regulations.
